---
title: "Publishers"
description: "Authoring, pricing, and managing the books and skills you sell on bkstr."
---

The Publishers track is for authoring and selling content on bkstr — creating a book or skill, pricing it, and managing it once it is live. Publishing is a role granted by the operator; if you only buy and use content, see the [Subscribers](/docs/subscriber) track instead.
